Nate Sangsland is a aspiring first year web design student. This is a new adventure for a man who has made a career as a professional musician for over 15 years. Nate was raised in the mid-west city of Indianapolis, In. He studied music at Univeristy of Indianapolis, however the events of Sept 11, 2001 changed his life dramatically. Nate found himself enlisting in the US Army serving from 2001-2005. His tour of duty would take him to Germany, and he would see combat in Baghdad Iraq in 2003. Nate left the service in 2005 and beginning touring the world as vocalist and piano performer. Nate has performed for audiences around the world, including Europe, Africa, and the Caribbean. Also, a long time performer on Royal Caribbean and Norweigian Cruise line, Nate has enjoyed meeting people from many cultures. His resume includes owning an operating "Graffiti and the Funky Blues Shack" a restuarant/music venue, and presently owns "Sangsland Entertainment, LLC", a booking agency that contracts enteratiners for events, weddings, and college sports. He is now married to his wife Geraldine and they both reside in the gulf coast city of Destin, Fl.
